Ingredients List

  • Platano/Plantains
  • Salami
  • Queso de freir/Cheese for frying
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Peel plantains and boil them.
  2. When plantains fully cooked, drain them of water.
  3. Mash the plantains, add milk and butter.
  4. Fry the salami
  5. Fry the cheese
  6. Fry the eggs
  7. Fry the onions
  8. Serve, enjoy!
